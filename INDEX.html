About Us

Tours-USA.com is a specialist tour operator offering the best holidays to the United States and Canada. We have a unique and comprehensive programme of escorted motorcoach tours, self-drive tours, city packages, cruises, rail tours and fly-drive holidays across North America, all at fantastic prices. Tours-USA.com team is made up of a diverse and enthusiastic group of people all sharing a deep passion for the United States and Canada, two of the most interesting, intriguing and beautiful countries in the world.
